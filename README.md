# Gym Calorie Weight Analysis

## Objective
Analyze the relationship between calorie intake and weight change over 30 days.

## Data
- Daily calorie intake
- Body weight
- Workout duration
- Protein intake
- Sleep

## Methods
- Data visualization
- Moving average (7-day)
- Linear regression

## Results
- Weight shows a downward trend with fluctuations
- Weak negative relationship between calories and weight
- Low R² indicates other factors affect weight change

## Limitations
- Small sample size
- Calorie tracking may be inaccurate
- Weight affected by water retention and other factors

## Conclusion
Calorie deficit contributes to weight loss, but results are not linear and are influenced by multiple variables.
